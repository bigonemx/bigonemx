Proximity

GoPro

GoPro.com

GoPro launched the new black hero 9 camera with a colorful campaign. The company updated the page with new ads like this one. This particular ad is an excellent example of proximity design. The camera, the sd card, and the number nine make a beautiful collage that includes the purchase.

Alignment

Apple

apple.com

The add that apple designed for the webpage is beautiful. The alignment between the centered text and the iPhone in the middle of the screen looks great. The image of the phone is more significant than the text but perfectly aligned for the screen.

Contrast

Stance

stance.com

The contrast of colors black, red, and white makes the ad look elegant and straightforward to understand. The background helps to contrast the colors in the mask and the buttons brightly.
